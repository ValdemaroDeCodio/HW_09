
                                Hello! How can I help you?

                                        Commands:

"add...": Please enter contact's name and phone number, separated by a space instead of the ellipsis (...).

"change...": This command allows you to change the phone number for an existing contact.
Please enter contact's name and phone number, separated by a space instead of the ellipsis (...).

"clear": For clear the phone book.

"phone...": Please enter the name of the contact whose number you want to display instead of (...).

"show all": To display all saved contacts with their phone numbers in the console.

"exit": To terminate the program.
